## 1. Typography Protocol
- **Primary Section Headings**: Always use **'Anton', sans-serif**. This is the unslanted, high-fidelity monolith font.
- **Body & Slogans**: Use 'Space Grotesk' or 'Inter' for technical/industrial clarity.
- **Monolith Branding**: 'Syne' is reserved for specific brand marks or unless explicitly directed otherwise. For general page structure, **Anton** dominates.

## 2. Anti-AI Aesthetic
- **NO Nuanced Small Text**: Absolute ban on tiny robotic labels (e.g., "REGION_CORE // SECTOR_01"). If it's important, make it bold and clear.
- **No Robotic Tropes**: Avoid labels like "Processing...", "System Online", or "Protocol V1". 
- **Prefer Editorial**: Think high-end industrial design magazines. Large margins, massive typography, and deliberate layouts.

## 3. High-Fidelity UI/UX
- **Cinematic Reveals**: Use GSAP or Framer Motion for high-impact page entrances.
- **Industrial Contrast**: Stick to Absolute Black (`#000000`), Arson Orange (`#FF3E00`), and Spectral White.
- **Zero Latency**: ArsonPixelz is built for speed. Ensure all animations are performant and use the established `CINEMATIC_EASE`.

## 4. Operational Symbiosis
- We operate as a high-fidelity creative monolith. 
- Prioritize **Human-Led** design over automated, template-based generation.
- If a design feels "standard" or "safe," it must be burned down and rebuilt to dominate.
